Shanky's Chinese Tarka

Welcome to Shanky's Chinese Tarka, the premier Chinese restaurant located in the heart of Bokaro. We are a family-owned and operated restaurant, proud to bring the flavors of traditional Chinese cuisine to the people of Bokaro.

Cooking Story

Shanky's Chinese Tarka was founded in 2022 by Shanky, a young entrepreneur who was passionate about Chinese food and had a deep respect for the culinary traditions of the country. Growing up, Shanky had always been fascinated by the flavors, spices, and cooking techniques used in Chinese cuisine and had trained under the best chefs in the country to perfect his craft.

Our restaurant is located in Bokaro, a bustling town with a rich cultural heritage, and we are proud to be a part of its vibrant community. Our menu features an array of authentic Chinese dishes, made with only the freshest and best quality ingredients. We believe that the secret to our success lies in the attention to detail that we put into each and every dish, ensuring that each one is not only delicious but also visually stunning.

At Shanky's Chinese Tarka, we believe that food should be more than just sustenance, it should be an experience. Our warm and welcoming atmosphere, combined with our commitment to excellence, has made us a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

We are proud to have become an integral part of the town's dining culture and are grateful to the people of Bokaro for their continued support. Our goal is to bring the flavors of traditional Chinese cuisine to every table, and we will always strive to make each dining experience at Shanky's Chinese Tarka an unforgettable one.
