Privacy Policy

Shanky's Chinese Tarka is committed to protecting the privacy of its customers and website visitors. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to ensure that your personal information is protected.

Information Collection

We may collect the following types of information from you when you visit our website:

  • Personal Information: This includes information such as your name, email address, telephone number, and other contact details. We may collect this information when you make a reservation or place an order through our website, or when you sign up to receive news and updates from us.
  • Payment Information: When you make a purchase through our website, we may collect information such as your credit card number, billing address, and other payment details. We use secure encryption technology to protect your payment information.
  • Usage Information: This includes information about how you use our website, such as the pages you visit and the actions you take. We use this information to improve our website and provide a better user experience.

    • Information Use
